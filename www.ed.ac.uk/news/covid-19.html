    Current advice:

    All Covid rules and restrictions have been lifted in Scotland. At this stage of the pandemic, Scottish Government advise that we are now living with Covid-19 as a respiratory infection.Â Read the latest guidance:

    Scottish Government website

    However, we can still play our part in keeping levels of infection down:

    • Clean your hands regularly
    • Respect other's personal space
    • Wear a face covering in indoor or crowded spaces
    • Stay home if you're unwell with a respiratory illness

    Advice and support

    People with symptoms are no longer advised that they need to arrange a Covid test.

    The public health advice for people who feel unwell is to stay at home until they feel better, to reduce the risk of infecting other people.
