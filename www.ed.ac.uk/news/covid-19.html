Latest update:

All Covid rules and restrictions have been lifted in Scotland. Read the latest guidance:

Scottish Government website

We are asking our whole University community to play your part and help stop the spread of Covid-19:

  • Clean your hands regularly
  • Respect other's personal space
  • Wear a face covering
  • Stay home if you're unwell

Play your part | Use TestEd  | Get vaccinated

Advice and support

From 1 May 2022, test sites will close and people with symptoms will no longer be advised that they need to arrange a Covid test.

The public health advice for people who feel unwell will be to stay at home until they feel better, to reduce the risk of infecting other people.
